Welcome to the Silly Geese!

What: A not-for-profit organization dedicated to event based community building in Boulder, CO and the Front Range.

Why: So many people today feel socially isolated. We weren't meant to live like this. We belong in small, tight knit community groups where the people know and care for each other. Let's build connections to create a strong and supportive community.

How: We plan events!! Our objective is to create fun and safe spaces for likeminded people to meet, connect, and develop meaningful relationships.

When/Where: It’s up to you! Events will be planned all year at regular intervals. There will be many different types of events to ensure that there’s something for everyone. Where we go and what we do is open to suggestions!! Events currently being considered include new member meet up, trivia nights, hikes, ski trips, rock climbing days, and more! Who: YOU! Join the WhatsApp Group for event coordination, sign ups, etc.
